Ingredients

Matcha powder

Water

Milk

Sweetener (optional)

Ice

Cold form (optional)

Instructions

1. Get your matcha powder

2. Boil Water

3. Grab a tea leaf strainer and put the powder in it and seperate clumps

4. Pour a bit of hot water into bowl

5. Use a bamboo whisk to mix the powder and water together until it becomes frothy

6. Add milk and sweetener to taste

7. Add ice if you want it cold

8. Enjoy your matcha!
